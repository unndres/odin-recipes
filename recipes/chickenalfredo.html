Creamy Chicken Alfredo

This chicken alfredo is like nothing that you have eaten ever before. With the first bite your mouth will be oozing with so much creamyness. The key ingredient is the cheesy paremesean cheese along with the heavy cream.

You need to make this recipe for your friends and family. They will be beyond impressed and will literally beg you for the recipe or for you to make it again and again.

ingredients

  • cheese
  • fettucine
  • onions
  • garlic
  • chicken
  • heavy cream

Recipe Steps

  1. Cut your chicken into julliene cuts and season to taste and fry them.
  2. Cook your pasta in boiling water.
  3. Fry some onions and garlic in a wide pan
  4. Add your heavy whipping cream to the pan along with the cheese.
  5. Keep stirring until your cream gets thick
  6. Add your chicken to your pasta bowl
  7. Finally mix in your delicious creamy sauce to your bowl
